Atakan Hilmi Fırat's Home Page

Welcome to my website! My name is Atakan Hilmi Fırat and I am a postdoctoral scholar at Center for Quantum Mathematics and Physics (QMAP) of University of California Davis. I have completed my Ph.D. in physics at Center for Theoretical Physics (CTP) of MIT under the supervision of Barton Zwiebach. I have earned my B.A. in physics and mathematics at University of Colorado Boulder. You can find my CV here. Feel free to reach me at ahfirat@ucdavis.edu.

My research focuses on string theory---string field theory (SFT) in particular, which is an attempt to formulate string theory nonperturbatively. I am still not entirely convinced whether this is the right approach for this problem to be honest; but this is the entire point of my research! Hope to see more progress in future. See here to read the summary of my research, together with my reflections and confessions that would have been quite inappropriate to include to the papers themselves. You can refer to Inspire, arXiv, or Google Scholar for the proper works.

This website is mostly academically-oriented. I also aim to share my personal thoughts on things that I find somewhat profound---not necessarily limited to physics---from time to time. Let us see what the future holds...
